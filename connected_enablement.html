Product Readiness Gap
New products (CoModeler, Agent Studio) launch with minimal enablement. Partners may go months post-GA without role-specific training available.
Role Coverage Imbalance
Delivery resources are reasonably served. Sales and Pre-Sales have almost no application-specific journeys — a direct impact on pipeline and deal quality.
Depth vs. Breadth
Most application courses are overview-level. Partners need delivery-ready, stage-specific content to reach Certified and Delivering — these are largely absent today.
